Lung Cancer Prevention

Preventive measures to reduce lung cancer risk

🚭 Avoid Tobacco & Smoking

Smoking is the leading cause of lung cancer. Quitting smoking significantly reduces risk, even if you have smoked for many years. Avoid second-hand smoke exposure as well.

🌫 Reduce Exposure to Pollutants

Limit exposure to air pollution, chemical fumes, asbestos, and industrial toxins. Use protective equipment if working in hazardous environments.

🥗 Maintain Healthy Lifestyle
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits and vegetables
  • Exercise regularly
  • Maintain a healthy weight
  • Stay hydrated
🩺 Regular Health Checkups

Early screening and regular medical checkups help detect abnormalities at an early stage. High-risk individuals should consult healthcare professionals regularly.

💡 Use AI for Early Risk Screening

AI-powered tools like this system help assess preliminary risk factors. However, they do not replace professional medical diagnosis. Always consult a certified doctor for confirmation.
