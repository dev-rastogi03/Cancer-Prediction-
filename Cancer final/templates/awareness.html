Lung Cancer Awareness

Understanding the risks, symptoms, and importance of early detection

What is Lung Cancer?

Lung cancer is a condition in which abnormal cells grow uncontrollably in the lungs. It is one of the leading causes of cancer-related deaths worldwide. Early detection significantly improves survival rates.

Major Risk Factors
  • Smoking and tobacco use
  • Exposure to second-hand smoke
  • Air pollution
  • Occupational exposure to chemicals
  • Family history of lung cancer
Common Symptoms
  • Persistent coughing
  • Chest pain
  • Shortness of breath
  • Fatigue
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Coughing up blood
Why Awareness is Important

Raising awareness helps in early diagnosis and prevention. Regular health screenings, avoiding tobacco, and maintaining a healthy lifestyle can significantly reduce risk. AI-based systems like this project assist in preliminary risk evaluation, but professional medical consultation is always recommended.
